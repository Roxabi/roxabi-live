## Problem

The Lyra Origin Talks trilogy (#450 dev/tech, #451 product/startup, #TBD general) needs its
second presentation. `/talks/lyra-dev` covers the journey through an MMORPG lens for a developer
audience. This talk retells the same 52-day journey — from a single Python script to a 6-repo
ecosystem — but through a **product/startup/indie-hacker lens**: decision-making speed, knowing
when to kill your darlings, the compounding value of shared infrastructure, and how an ecosystem
emerges from one person moving fast with clear principles.

The source material (`histoire-2ndbrain-lyra.md`) is dense and complete. The challenge is
distillation: ~600 lines of events into 18 scroll-snap sections with a coherent arc that resonates
with people who care about iteration velocity, product decisions, and building things that outlast
the first version.

## Who

- **Primary:** Startup founders, indie hackers, product-minded developers — people who build
  products, not just features. Attend product meetups, follow makers on X.
- **Secondary:** Anyone who's pivoted, killed a feature they loved, or watched a side project
  evolve into something they didn't plan.

## Constraints

- Route: `/talks/lyra-product` (new route, separate from existing `/lyra-dev` and `/lyra-story`)
- Pattern: follow `lyra-dev` architecture exactly — `SectionContainer` + `SectionChrome` +
  `PresentationNav` + scroll-snap + `LyraCompanion` avatar companion
- i18n: FR + EN, all copy in `apps/web/messages/{locale}.json`, keys prefixed `talk_lp_*`
- Visual identity: product/startup aesthetic — warmer, more human than lyra-dev's terminal green.
  Use amber/orange accent (decision-making urgency) on a near-dark background. Think "deck
  slides meets maker diary" rather than MMORPG terminal.
- No mode toggle (unlike lyra-story) — single mode, product narrative throughout
- Companion stage: raw `currentSectionIndex` (like lyra-dev, simpler than lyra-story)
- ~18 sections, no divider (uniform sections, unlike lyra-story's AwakeningDivider)
- Source: `histoire-2ndbrain-lyra.md` for content
